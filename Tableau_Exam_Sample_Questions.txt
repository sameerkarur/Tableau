1. **Average Exam Score**: What is the average exam score for all candidates?
2. **Pass Rate by Country**: What is the pass rate for candidates from each country?
3. **Training Completion Rate**: What percentage of candidates have completed training?
4. **Experience Distribution**: How is the candidates' experience (in months) distributed?
5. **Preferred Exam Mode**: What is the most preferred exam mode among candidates (Online vs. Onsite)?

### Intermediate Questions

9. **Pass Rate Over Time**: How has the pass rate changed over different exam attempt dates?
10. **Training Impact on Scores**: Analyze the impact of training completion on exam scores.

### Advanced Questions
